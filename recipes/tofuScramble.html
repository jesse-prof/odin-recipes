Tofu Scramble

Description

This is a delicious scrambled tofu recipe that uses nutritional yeast, black salt, turmeric, and garlic powder. For added creaminess, non-dairy milk is stirred in. Once done, this scramble is served warm alongside toast, avocado, berries, or whatever other breakfast favorites you may have in mind

Ingredients

  • Olive oil or other oil/vegan butter
  • Firm or extra-firm tofu
  • Nutritional yeast
  • Black salt (kala namak)
  • Turmeric
  • Garlic powder
  • Unsweetened, unflavored non-dairy milk

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Mash or crumble the tofu directly in the pan.
  3. Cook, stirring often, until excess water evaporates.
  4. Sprinkle nutritional yeast, black salt, turmeric, and garlic powder over the tofu.
  5. Cook and stir the seasoned tofu for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour in the non-dairy milk and stir to combine.
  7. Serve warm with your favorite breakfast sides like avocado, toast, or berries.
