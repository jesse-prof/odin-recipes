Banana Bread

Description

This almond-flour banana bread is moist and flavorful. It is made with mashed bananas, almond flour, and warm spices like cinnamon. Sweetened naturally and baked to tenderness, it’s perfect for breakfast, a snack, or dessert. It pairs well with butter or any of your preferred toppings.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Almond Flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon and Pure Vanilla Extract
  • Sweetener

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan with gluten-free cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, mash bananas until smooth, then stir in baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt until combined.
  3. Add eggs, vanilla extract, and your preferred sweetener, mixing well. Fold in almond flour until fully incorporated.
  4. Transfer the thick batter to the prepared pan, smoothing the top, and bake on the center rack for about 50 minutes—oven times may vary, so keep an eye on it.
  5. Let the bread cool completely before slicing and serving.
  6. Store leftovers in an airtight container, or freeze the cooled loaf wrapped in foil or plastic wrap inside a freezer bag for up to three months.
